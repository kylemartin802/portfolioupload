EPSRC Research Intern - Lancaster University [2019]

As an EPSRC Intern I worked alongside HCI researchers from the AffecTech research network to develop haptic prototypes for meditation using Arduino microcontrollers. I documented the research, design, implementation and evaluation processes, and ultimately produced three distinct prototypes. I enjoyed learning about the challenges of hardware prototyping and found the experimental nature of the work engaging. This internship was completed remotely.
